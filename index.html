How It Works

Upvote is designed to give your favorite content a boost with just one click. It finds all upvote buttons on supported platforms and clicks them simultaneously, ensuring you never miss a chance to support the posts you love.

Step 1: Install

Add Upvote to your browser from the extension store.

Step 2: Click

Activate the extension whenever you want to upvote content.

Step 3: Boost

Sit back and watch as the extension clicks all upvote buttons across your favorite platform!
